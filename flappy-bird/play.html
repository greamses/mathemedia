Math Flappy Bird

Game Objective: Answer math problems correctly to keep flying!

Controls:

  • Jump: Press SPACE, UP ARROW, or click/tap the screen
  • Answer Questions: Click the correct answer or press 1-4 to select

Game Rules:

  • Choose a table to practice at the start
  • Answer problems correctly to get points
  • Wrong answers will end the game
  • Avoid hitting the pipes or the ground

Tips:

  • Time your jumps carefully to navigate through pipes
  • Focus on the math problems while avoiding obstacles
  • The game gets faster as you score more points
